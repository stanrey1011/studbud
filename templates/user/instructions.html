📖 Study Modes

🎓 Study Mode

Best for: Learning new material

  • Immediate feedback after each question
  • Shows correct answers and explanations
  • No time pressure
  • Review mistakes instantly
💡 Tip: Use this mode when first encountering new topics.
🔄 Flashcard Mode

Best for: Quick review and memorization

  • Question on front, answer on back
  • Click to reveal answers
  • Perfect for terminology
  • Fast-paced learning
💡 Tip: Great for reviewing before exams.
⏱️ Simulation Mode

Best for: Exam practice

  • Timed test environment
  • No immediate feedback
  • Customizable time limits
  • Final score at the end
💡 Tip: Use this to simulate real exam conditions.

🏆 Tips for Success

Study Strategy

  • 📚 Start with Study Mode
    Learn the material with immediate feedback
  • 🔄 Use Flashcards for Review
    Quick reinforcement of key concepts
  • ⏱️ Practice with Simulations
    Build exam-taking confidence
  • 📈 Track Your Progress
    Review history to identify weak areas

Exam Tips

  • 🎯 Read Questions Carefully
    Pay attention to keywords like "best," "most," "least"
  • 🖼️ Study Topology Images
    Network diagrams often contain important clues
  • ✅ For MRQ Questions
    Remember: you need ALL correct answers
  • 🔗 For Match Questions
    Think about relationships between terms and definitions
